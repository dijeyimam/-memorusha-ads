Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

MemoruSha Academy ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information and user data.

1. Target Audience (Age Restriction)

This service is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take steps to delete it.

2. Data Collection & Storage

The app does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data (such as name, email, or photos) to our servers. All training progress, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services & Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and process limited data based on your consent, including:

  • Advertising ID (GAID)
  • Device information (model, OS, network)
  • Approximate location (based on IP)
  • Ad interactions (impressions, clicks)

4. User Consent (GDPR/UK/EEA)

For users in regulated regions (EEA, UK), we use a Consent Management Platform (CMP). You will be asked for permission before personalized ads are shown.

  • If you consent: Google may use your Advertising ID to show relevant (personalized) ads.
  • If you decline: You will still see ads, but they may be less relevant (non-personalized). Your Advertising ID will not be used for personalization.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time in the app settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not sell or share personal data under CCPA/CPRA.

We may share limited data with service providers (such as AdMob) to deliver and measure ads. Advertising identifiers (AD_ID) may be processed by ad service providers for advertising and measurement.

6. Permissions

The app uses the following permission to function compliantly:

  • Advertising ID Permission (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID): Used by Google AdMob for advertising and measurement.

7. Security

We prioritize the security of your local data. Since we do not transmit your personal training data to any cloud server, your progress remains solely on your device. Please note that no electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. International Use

The app is available globally. We do not transmit or store user data on our servers. Ad providers may process data in different regions for ad delivery and measurement.

9. Your Rights (CCPA/GDPR)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data and to withdraw consent for personalized ads. Since we do not store user data on external servers, please contact the third-party service provider (Google) for ad-related requests, or use device/app options to reset local data.
