Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

MemoruSha Academy ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Target Audience (Age Restriction)

This Service is intended for general audiences aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

2. Data Collection & Storage

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data (such as name, email, or photos) to our servers. All training progress (PAO lists, scores, settings) is stored locally on your device.

However, we use third-party services (Google AdMob) that may collect technical data to provide advertisements.

3. Third-Party Services & Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process the following data based on your consent:

  • Advertising ID (GAID)
  • Device information (Model, OS, Network)
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interactions (Impressions, Clicks)

4. User Consent (GDPR/UK/EEA)

For users in regulated regions (EEA, UK), we use a Consent Management Platform (CMP). You will be asked for permission before personalized ads are shown.

  • If you Consent: Google uses your Advertising ID to show relevant ads.
  • If you Decline: You will see non-personalized ads, and your ID will not be used for personalization.

You can withdraw your consent at any time in the app settings.

5. Permissions

The app requires the following permission to function compliantly:

  • Advertising ID Permission (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID): Used by Google AdMob for advertising and analytics.
