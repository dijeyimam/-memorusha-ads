The Manifesto: Why I Built This?

Most "brain training" apps are just games filled with distractions. I needed a serious tool to practice PAO (Person-Action-Object), Anzan (Mental Calculation), and Speed Cards.

So I built MemoruSha Academy.

  • Offline First: No loading screens, works everywhere.
  • Local Architecture: No account required. Your training data stays on your device.
  • Global: Supports 7 languages including JP, CN, DE, ES.

Core Modules

1. PAO System: Master the technique used by memory champions.
2. Anzan Starter: Soroban-style mental math flash visualization.
3. Binary & Numbers: High-speed processing drills.
