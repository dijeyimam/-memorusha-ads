The Manifesto: Why I Built This?

Most "brain training" apps are just candy-crush clones filled with intrusive ads and fake science. I needed a serious tool to practice PAO (Person-Action-Object), Anzan (Mental Calculation), and Speed Cards.

So I built MemoruSha.

  • Offline First: No loading screens.
  • Privacy First: No data collection. Your brain, your data.
  • Global: Supports 7 languages including JP, CN, DE, ES.

Core Modules

1. PAO System: Master the technique used by memory champions.
2. Anzan Starter: Soroban-style mental math flash visualization.
3. Binary & Numbers: High-speed processing drills.
